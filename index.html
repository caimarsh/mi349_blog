Professional Events Happening in the Fraternity

Caitlyn Marsh
Oct, 30, 2022

Alpha Kappa Psi has had a lot of professional events this passed summer and during the semester so far. Over the summer, two of our brothers attending the AKPsi Convention in Miami, Florida. These brothers, Caitlyn Marsh and Zach Evans, voted on legislature, sat in on important brotherhood meetings, connected with alumni, and attended social events to bond with brothers. This fall semester has been a busy one with numerous professional events already in effect. Last week, TTI came and sproke to the brothers at the house about opportunities within the company. This upcoming week, Ratheon is hosting a virtual meeting for brothers to learn about their company. After this event, a few other business fratnertities collaborated with AKPsi to have a career fair, bringing in numerous new companies, giving all our brothers access to professional opportunities. More professional events are in the works so stay tuned for more blog posts!

Fun Things Happening in the Fratnernity

Caitlyn Marsh
Oct, 30, 2022

On top of all things professional, Aplha Kappa Psi hosts social events for the brothers to enjoy. This past week, we had Big Little Night to celebrate our new family pairings. The Bigs unveiled who they were to the New Members, they were all so excited and happy! After this, we celebrated with family traditions and brotherhood bonding. The next night, one of our families put on a comedy night, a suprise for the brotherhood. Friends acted skits and a few brothers even did stand up. Many laughs were had and smiles were on everyones faces. This weekend at MSU was homecoming! We hosted an event at the house to celebrate all of our brothers coming back to campus. We connected with alumni new brothers had not met yet and reconnected with alumni we have know for years! This was an amazing time for everyone, we were happy to see familiar faces on campus again. Look out for next weeks blog post on more social events!
